Riffer Madness

An iOS application for musical brainstorming.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

What We Collect

Riffer Madness does not collect any personal information. All audio recordings, Riffs, Compositions, and user data are stored exclusively on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

Audio Data

Audio is captured from your device’s microphone or imported from files you select. This audio data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the share or export feature. We cannot access your recordings.

Third-Party Services

Riffer Madness does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in the App’s sandbox on your iOS device. This data is included in your iCloud or iTunes backups if you have those enabled on your device.

Children’s Privacy

Riffer Madness does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the App collects no personal data at all, there is no children’s data to protect.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@riffermadness.app.
